The roleplaying game Saga Shuffle, that is.

Here, players collaborate on the setting, plot, and characters of the game using saga cards as basic building blocks and then roleplay the tale using the regular (“poker”) deck.

There are five stages to play:
~Setup
~Scenario
~Game Play
~Interaction
~Effects

The saga cards divided into three groups:
~Character
~Setting
~Plot

This game can be played by yourself or with up to seven other people.
