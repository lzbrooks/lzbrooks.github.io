The Parts of the Character Explained:
Origin

Base the character’s personal history off this.
~Standard: Badass normal. No inborn abilities.
~Off-beat: Born shape-shifter.
~Psionic: Born with mental powers.
~Magician: Learned ritual magic.  Requires outside sources of power, but can be used by anyone.
~Tech: Uses advanced technology.  Up to and including cyborg additions.
~Mod: Has been genetically modified by science or magic.

Animal

Use as a base for the character’s physical traits and symbolism.

Power

Design the abilities of the character using these. Adjust for more mundane settings.

Character Traits

Develop the personality traits of the character off these.
